Emotional Intelligence Module

Our key innovation is the Emotional Intelligence module that processes:

  • Biometric Data: Heart rate, sleep quality, movement patterns
  • Speech Patterns: Tone, pace, energy level, word choice
  • Facial Expressions: Micro-expressions, eye movement, smile detection
  • Behavioral Cues: Walking speed, interaction frequency, activity patterns

The system calculates key metrics including:

  • Fatigue level (0-100)
  • Stress level (0-100)
  • Comfort level (0-100)
  • Dominant emotion (joy, interest, surprise, distress, fear, anger)
  • Emotional intensity (0-100)
